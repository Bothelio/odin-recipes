BEEF


Found and ground fresh for the consumption of all.

Ingredients

  • small cow
  • brocollini
  • carrots (baby or adult)
  • butter
  • salt + pep

Steps

  • slaughter cow and remove prime rib
  • season with salt and pepper
  • braise carrots in butter and season
  • add brocollini to carrots
  • grill prime rib
  • serve
